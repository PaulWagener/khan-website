Setting up a class

To get the most out of Khan Academy, you'll need to set up student and teacher accounts. We've created a handy guide to make sure you'll be up and running in no time.

We recommend setting up Google accounts to register classes on Khan Academy. You can register accounts through Google Apps for Education.

  • Google Apps for Education registration
  • Google Apps for Education common questions

A smooth start to class

To get started easily, we recommend pre-registering students for class with assigned Google accounts (e.g., john-appleseed@school.org for the student John Appleseed) and passwords (e.g., school2012). Students can use these email addresses and passwords to log into KA with Google.

Logging in to a new system for the first time can be a bit hectic, so we included a sample handout to guide students through the initial login process.

Coaches can see students' data and help support their learning. If you want to see your students' data, you'll need to be their coach. We also encourage students to add their parents as coaches for extra support. To add a coach to their accounts, students should follow the instructions below:
