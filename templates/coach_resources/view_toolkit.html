Setting up accounts

In order to get the most out of Khan Academy, you'll need to set up student and teacher accounts. We've created a handy guide to make sure you'll be up and running in no time.

Your school will need to be set up with Google accounts to register classes in the Khan Academy. You can register accounts through Google Apps for Education.

  • Google Apps for Education registration
  • Google Apps for Education common questions

In order to set up student accounts, follow the instructions below:

Each of your students needs to sign in and follow these steps:

  1. First, they should click on the Add a Coach link at the bottom of the page.

  2. Then, they should enter your Coach ID in the form that looks like this:

    {{ coach_email|escape }}

    Your Coach ID is {{ coach_email|escape }}.

  3. Finally, they need to click the Register Coach button. It looks like this:

    {{ coach_email|escape }}

Once students make you their coach, you'll have access to all types of interesting data about their progress and achievements.

You can access that data via the Class Report.
